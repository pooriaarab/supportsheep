"It killed the spreadsheet" — Aaliyah Khan on switching to BlogBat

by Pooria Arab · featuring Aaliyah Khan · drafted from your conversation

Aaliyah Khan ran content ops at Acme Co. for two years before her team picked up BlogBat. She remembers Monday standups as "three hours of figuring out what was already on the backlog spreadsheet, which nobody trusted."

The first week with BlogBat, those three hours collapsed into a 15-minute review of what the system had drafted overnight. "It killed the spreadsheet," she said.
